Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

This policy explains what information Florida Liquor License Market collects through this website, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to provide through contact, buyer, seller, financing, private-lender, and offer forms. This may include your name, email address, telephone number, county, license information, transaction preferences, proposed prices or financing amounts, source-of-funds categories, and messages.

When you submit a paid listing, payment information is collected and processed by Stripe. We receive transaction details needed to match and review the submission, but we do not receive or store your complete payment-card number.

Our hosting and service providers may automatically process limited technical information such as IP address, browser or device type, referring page, timestamps, and security or diagnostic data.

How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested marketplace services.
  • Review, publish, administer, and verify license listings.
  • Connect potential buyers, sellers, financing sources, or private lenders when appropriate.
  • Process and reconcile listing-submission payments.
  • Prevent spam, fraud, misuse, and security incidents.
  • Maintain records, improve the website, and comply with legal obligations.

When Information Is Shared

We may share information with service providers that support website hosting, form delivery, communications, payment processing, security, and professional services. Financing or lender-interest information may be shared with potential transaction participants only when reasonably necessary to evaluate or facilitate the opportunity.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights or safety, in connection with a business transfer, or with your direction or consent. We do not sell personal information for money.

Payments and External Services

Stripe and other external services process information under their own privacy policies and terms. Review Stripe's Privacy Policy before completing payment. Links to third-party listings or resources are governed by the third party's policies.

Retention and Security

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, to resolve disputes, and to satisfy legal, accounting, or operational requirements. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no website, email, or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your Choices

You may ask to review, correct, or delete information you submitted, subject to legal and operational retention requirements. You may also ask us not to share a pending financing or lender inquiry with potential transaction participants.

Children

This website is intended for adults involved in business and investment transactions and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests may be submitted through our Contact page. We may update this policy as the marketplace changes; the effective date above identifies the current version.
